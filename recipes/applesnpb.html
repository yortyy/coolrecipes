Apples And Peanut Butter

This cool recipe is an awesome snack solution, Apples with peanut butter.

The apples contains great nutrients, with peanut butter providing a great amout of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 5-8 Tbsps Peanut Butter
  • 3-5 tsps of Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the apple, then cut into snackable slices
  2. Serve slices on plate, with peanut butter in a tray on the side
  3. Using a spoon, get about a tbps of peanut butter and spread on the apple slices
  4. Eat!
